ESEM

Chapter 1: Vacation.....SM?

Ivan is on vacation after a year of suffering in school.
He thinks of places he wanted to go while browsing with his phone. After an hour ofn thinking, he decided to
go and spend his first day of vacation in Esem (SM) since its cheaper to travel than any other places.
After a long ride of jeepney, he arrived at SM, he took a deep breath and muttered "I missed this place".
He checks every store; clothing store, tech store, and department store. He didn't buy anything; he just look at the items that
might be worth of buying. After a long walk, he decided to go eat at Jollibee. On his way there, he saw his classmante Juana
sitting on a bench crying.

Chapter 2: Jollibee but not Jolly?

Ivan approaced Juana, once he called Juana's name
she looks at him with tears in her eyes. They sat on that bench, Ivan already forgot that he is hungry, started to listen to Juana ranting about her day.
She starts ranting about a guy she met in the school's library, they talked and developed feelings. Juana took a deep breath before continuing.
One week before vacation... she asked the dude to go out with her but at the last minute he cancels it and go with someone else. To Ivan, she is just overreacting, she stopped Juana
from ranting and invited her to go with him at Jollibee. Once they're in there, she keeps ranting about the guy, and started crying loud again once they are eating. In Ivan's mind, he thinks that she took
the jolly in Jollibee.

Chapter 3: I somehow made her day?

After eating, Ivan and Juana, sat in front of an arcade. She is calm now and not ranting anything anymore.
Ivan, grabbed Juana's hand and went to the arcade. They played a lot of games like Whack a Mole, Tekken, and The Claw Machine. After that, Juana asked Ivan
why did you treat me to that arcade. He just replies, "I just want to cheer you up, that's what friends do right?"
Juana smiled then they proceeded doing a lot of fun things. They ended their day with a small picture inside a photobooth.
Ivan keeps complaining that they don't need it, but Juana insisted that they should took one. After that on his way home Ivan received a message from Juana thanking him for making her day fun and asking if when they could hangout again. Ivan smiled and muttered, "I'm glad I went to Esem."
